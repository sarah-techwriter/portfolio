How I Use Claude and NotebookLM to Accelerate My Learning and Technical Writing Process

This is my learning journey blog on "how I leverage AI tools Claude and NotebookLM to accelerate my learning, streamline technical writing creation, and produce higher-quality documentation."

Written by Sarah • 8 min read

My AI-Powered Learning Journey 

As a person with high curiosity on random topics and an enjoyment for trying new tools and software, I've developed a systematic approach using AI tools that completely accelerates my learning journey and technical writing project. My process evolved from experimenting with various AI chatbot tools and ultimately combines the research power of NotebookLM with Claude's writing assistance.

AI usage has genuinely surprised me. I was a late adopter—people around me were already using these tools while I was still skeptical. I experienced firsthand both the negative perceptions people have about AI and its actual potential to accelerate productivity. I thought this AI phenomenon was out of my control, like I had no choice: either get left behind while people adapt to AI, or learn how AI chatbots actually work.

Now I see AI as helpful tools, along with understanding AI ethics and using them responsibly without dishonesty. Here's my systematic approach to using AI for accelerated learning and assisted writing:

Phase 1: Research and Knowledge Building

NotebookLM for Deep Learning

Why NotebookLM became my research foundation:

  • Zero hallucination risk, it only provides responses based on the sources you upload and store
  • Accepts any source format, supported file types include PDF, txt, Markdown, Audio (e.g., mp3), and various mediums: Google Drive, websites, YouTube links, or even copied text from your notes
  • Source transparency, always shows which document supports each claim, so you can directly verify where information came from
  • Supports multiple learning styles, whether you're a visual, auditory, reading/writing, or VARK learner. Features include audio overview, video overview, mind maps, reports, flashcards, quizzes, and direct notes are provided.

The benefits:

  • Research acceleration, synthesizes multiple sources instantly
  • Confidence in accuracy, no hallucination concerns (compared to regular AI chatbots)
  • Comprehensive coverage, identifies concepts I might miss

My Research Process:

  1. Gather authoritative sources, I hunt down topic-related sources from across the internet. When I can't locate specific sources, I use NotebookLM's discover feature to easily add relevant materials
  2. Generate study guides, let it create comprehensive overviews in their studio
  3. Extract key concepts, identify what I need to understand deeply

Claude for Technical Exploration

Why Claude gives me the most outstanding output compared to other AI chatbots:

  • Excellent outlining, helps me create logical information architectures with clear structure and understandable purpose
  • Consistent explanations, maintains minimal hallucination across my daily contextual discussions
  • Straight-to-the-point responses, no fluff, just what I need
  • Context understanding, catch my knowledge level with simple prompts

The Benefits:

  • Consistent quality, maintains accuracy
  • User-focused writing, organizes information for optimal understanding
  • Efficient iteration, quick refinements based on feedback

Phase 2: Content Creation and Drafting

My Hybrid Workflow

Step 1: Foundation Building (NotebookLM)

  • Research topic thoroughly using stored sources
  • Generate comprehensive study materials
  • Identify key concepts that need clear explanation

Step 2: Structure Development (Markdown or preferred notes)

  • Create content outlines based on exact needs
  • Develop logical information flow
  • Architecture information and draft freely

Step 3: Initial Draft (Claude)

  • Generate first draft with specific requirements
  • Enhance explanations through technical curiosity discussions with me

Step 4: Refinement (Claude + manually)

  • Polish language and clarity based on your exact needs
  • Ensure consistency across sections while discussing pain points
  • Optimize for target audience understanding
  • Language adjustment to match my writing style (manually)

Phase 3: Fact-Checking and Verification Methods

My Quality Control System

Primary validation (always required):

  • Source validation, cross-reference against original documentation
  • Hands-on testing, try the procedures and documentation myself
  • Logic checking, ensure explanations make technical sense
  • Words adjustment, refine few context to match preferred writing style
  • Multiple source confirmation, verify critical information through professional sources or experienced writers for feedback

Continuous Improvement

What I track:

  • Which prompt styles generate the most accurate initial content
  • Common verification points that need extra attention

Red flags I watch for:

  • Overly generic explanations (indicates I need better prompting)
  • Missing context about limitations or edge cases
  • Output that cannot be easily tested or verified

Process evolution: My prompts have become more specific and context-rich over time, leading to better outputs and fewer revisions.

Why This Approach Actually Works

The point is, AI chatbot matches my learning style. AI supports my high curiosity to discuss and dive deeper into interesting topics. I like learning through discussion, others' opinion is a new perspective to be considered, and it helps me explore and discover concepts I find unclear and unlogical, which I might not have considered before.

This methodology transforms my natural curiosity about complex topics into a systematic advantage, allowing me to master new technical domains quickly while maintaining the accuracy and user focus essential for effective writing.

Final Thoughts

I believe every AI has its own "personality" based on how they were trained and built. I use different AI tools for different contexts; some topics work better with GPT responses, others with Gemini Pro, Copilot, Perplexity AI, or even DeepSeek. The key is understanding each tool's strengths and applying them appropriately.
