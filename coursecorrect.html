    CourseCorrect Quick Start: Find Your Perfect Course in 5 Minutes

    Last updated: Sept, 2025

    Independent disclaimer: This user guide is independently created and not officially endorsed by CourseCorrect. Information is based on user experience and may become outdated as the web platform evolves. For official support, please refer to CourseCorrect's official website and channels.

    Table of Contents

      1. What is CourseCorrect?

      CourseCorrect is an AI-powered course matchmaker designed to help ambitious learners find the right upskilling course to move their careers forward. CourseCorrect's AI (called Cora) matches users with the best fit for their specific goals, skills, and experience level. The platform scans over 150,000 courses across platforms like Udemy, Coursera, and EdX, comparing content, reviews, pricing, and skills taught to make personalized recommendations.

      2. Sign Up

      Step-by-step registration

      1. Go to the official website of CourseCorrect
      2. Click the Google sign-in icon ("g" icon) on the top-right corner of the website.
      3. Select your Google business or educational account.
      4. Review and accept the agreement to access your name and email address.
      5. Click "continue" to proceed with account creation.

      3. Find Your First Course

      Search functionality

      • Enter a skill or topic in the main search bar.
      • Example: Enter "digital marketing" and press Enter.
      • The platform will provide auto-suggestions as you type.
      • Press Enter or click the search button to execute the query.

      4. Get AI Help

      How to access Cora

      • On desktop, click the chat bar on the dashboard's right side.
      • On mobile, tap the chat widget at the bottom-right corner.
      • Click "Help Me Choose" for guided questions about your search.
      • You can also type your own questions, such as: "What is the best course for a beginner learning Python?"

      5. Save Courses You Like

      Saving for later

      Click the save/bookmark button on any course card to add it to your saved courses list for later review. You can find this list by clicking your Google account icon in the top-right corner.
