CourseCorrect: Troubleshooting Guide

Troubleshooting

Account Issues

Problem Symptom(s) Possible Cause(s) Solution(s)
Login Failure Google sign-in button not working, authentication fails Personal Google account used, pop-up blocked, multiple accounts
  1. Ensure using a business/educational Google account
  2. Enable pop-ups for CourseCorrect
  3. Clear browser cache and cookies
Unsupported Account "Account type not supported" message appears Using a personal Gmail account instead of a business/educational one Switch to a valid business/educational Google account for login
Stuck on Permissions Process freezes after clicking "continue" on the permissions screen Declined access permissions, corporate firewall is blocking the connection
  1. Re-authorize and accept name/email permissions
  2. Check corporate firewall settings to allow CourseCorrect

Feature Usage Problems

Problem Symptom(s) Possible Cause(s) Solution(s)
Bad Recommendations Cora or search results provide irrelevant course suggestions Vague search terms, overly restrictive filters, unclear conversation with Cora
  1. Use more specific search keywords
  2. Clear filters and re-apply them carefully
  3. Give Cora detailed prompts about your goals and background
No Search Results The results page is empty or shows "No courses found" Search query is too niche, contains typos, or filters are too specific
  1. Check your search query for spelling errors
  2. Broaden your search terms
  3. Remove all filters and try the search again
Filters Not Applying Results don't change after selecting filters Forgot to click the "Update" button, or a minor browser glitch
  1. Ensure you click "Update" after making selections
  2. Refresh the page and try again

Data & Progress Issues

Problem Symptom(s) Possible Cause(s) Solution(s)
Saved Courses Missing Bookmarked courses do not appear in your saved list Not logged in, browser cache issue, or temporary sync error
  1. Ensure you are logged into your account
  2. Log out and log back in to refresh the session
  3. Clear your browser's cache
Outdated Information Course price or details are different on the provider's website There can be a slight delay in data synchronization from partner platforms Always trust the information on the official course provider's website as the most current source

Performance Issues

Problem Symptom(s) Possible Cause(s) Solution(s)
General Slowness Platform pages load slowly, search takes a long time to respond High server traffic, slow local internet connection, too many browser tabs open
  1. Check your internet connection speed
  2. Clear your browser cache and close unnecessary tabs
  3. Try accessing the platform during off-peak hours
